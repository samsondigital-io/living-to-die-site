## Quick Setup Instructions

1. **Right-click** on each organization's logo on their website
2. **Save image** to `public/images/resources/`
3. **Update the src** in `/src/pages/resources.astro`
4. **Replace placeholders** with real paths like `/images/resources/logo-name.png`

## Legal Note
Make sure to check each organization's media guidelines for logo usage. Most nonprofits encourage sharing their logos for awareness purposes, but it's good practice to review their media kits.
